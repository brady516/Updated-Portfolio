Earned, not overnight

Two decades to earn the outlook

The guru model takes a weekend and a ring light. This took twenty years:

  • 13 years as a trading amateur — self-taught, real money, real mistakes, before anyone handed me a credential.
  • MBA in investments, then FINRA Series 7 and Series 63.
  • Series 66 (which satisfies the Series 65 requirement) added later.
  • A decade in financial services across Reg D / private placements, retail, and professional risk management.

That's what earns the right to an opinion here — not confidence, not a thumbnail. Two decades of skin in the game, half of it before the first license. The receipts came after the reps.

The creed

I'll hold stocks, bonds, and options in my own accounts at 90. I will not outsource my wealth — not because advisors are villains, but because no one guards your money like the person who earned it. That's not advice for you. It's the conviction North de Noise is built on.
