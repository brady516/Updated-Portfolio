About Me

Looking for someone determined to make a positive impact on your business? Brady Gallagher is the first call you should make.

Brady Gallagher, MBA, is an accomplished business professional with a strong entrepreneurial spirit. He has seasoned experience with Merrill, Morgan Stanley Smith Barney, and Fidelity Investments — managing all aspects of financial advisory including investment-market intelligence, cash-flow analysis, equity trading, dividend-growth models, leveraging capital markets, goal-based planning, fiscal operations, business-model development, and novel go-to-market strategies built with the C-Suite.

His expertise spans multiple industries — retail, industrial, e-commerce, Bio-Tech, Fin-Tech, hospitality, consumer goods — including unicorn start-up experience.

Brady has led IPO and M&A opportunities, managing transactions that drove $8–13MM in firm revenue. He developed strategies and plans to grow and scale company revenue toward $87MM and $22MM at ~22% CAGR annually by year three.

His technical skills span Bloomberg, Lean Six Sigma, CRM, MoneyGuidePro, screening, code development & debugging, CSS, APIs, Git/GitHub, Google Workspace, Microsoft Office Suite, A/P processing, A/R reconciliation, GAAP statement development, systems implementation, and application development.

Brady holds a Master of Business Administration in Finance & Entrepreneurship from the University of Florida (GPA 3.71) and a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (Hospitality Management) from Coastal Carolina University. He was admitted to the Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts corporate training program, developing operations expertise across Ruth's Chris, Omni Hotels, and Ponte Vedra Beach Resorts. He holds FINRA Securities credentials (SIE, Series 7, Series 63, Series 66 — CRD 6510444) and an MD Life & Health Insurance License. He is trained in Python for algorithmic trading and financial analysis, quantitative trading, automated trading, and Solidity development, and built ASTI ("Skynet"), an Automated Screener and Trading Interface on Python connected to the IBKR API using a mean-reversion strategy. He also completed a duplex renovation achieving a 106% cash-on-cash return. (Results referenced are historical; past performance does not indicate or guarantee future results.)

Brady grew up in Sacramento, California, and now lives in Baltimore, MD with his wife, Marcie, and their daughters. He is an avid beer brewer and wine collector, and in season you'll find him polishing his surfing or working his strategic game on the golf course.
