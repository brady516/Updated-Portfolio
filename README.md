## The standard

Everything here is **educational, not individualized investment advice**; disclaimers
stay straight, performance is labeled historical with no guarantees, conflicts are
disclosed, and credentials are verifiable (FINRA BrokerCheck, CRD 6510444). The voice
is irreverent; the facts are not. *Strategic irreverence, grounded in virtue.*
