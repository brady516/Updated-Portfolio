# The Signal — newsletter strategy

> *Institutional-grade research. No minimums. No noise.*

The paid newsletter is Brady's lead cashflow vehicle: lowest cost to produce,
recurring revenue, and the most direct rebuttal to the "YouTube scourge" — a
paywall is a noise filter.

## Positioning

- **Audience:** pro-grade for everyone — institutional-quality analysis written
  so a motivated amateur can follow. "Merrill research without the Merrill
  minimum."
- **The wedge:** verifiable practitioner (FINRA BrokerCheck / CRD 6510444, real
  track record) holding himself to a self-imposed Rule 2210 standard — balanced
  risk, no guarantees, conflicts disclosed. The opposite of anonymous hype.
- **Cadence:** weekly. Consistency beats volume; a missed cadence kills paid
  newsletters.

## The weekly format (one repeatable template)

Writing weekly is only sustainable with a fixed structure. Every issue:

1. **The Read** — one market/idea explained at desk quality. *The meat; the paid
   deep section.*
2. **Signal vs. Noise** — teardown of one bad finance take, rebuilt with the math
   it left out. *The recurring differentiator; free hook.*
3. **The Number** — one chart/data point that actually matters this week.
4. **What I'd Watch** — practitioner's lens, framed as observation, **not advice**.
5. **Disclaimer** — the standard educational/not-advice footer (see any issue).

## Free vs. paid split (the conversion engine)

- **Free, every week (above the fold):** intro + *Signal vs. Noise* + *The
  Number*. Shareable, proves quality, grows the list.
- **Paid (below the fold):** *The Read* deep analysis, model walk-throughs (tied
  to the [store](../store.html)), subscriber Q&A, full archive, store discounts.
- **One fully-paid flagship per month** — a longer research report.

## Pricing

- **$49 / month** or **$550 / year** (annual saves ~$38).
- **Founding member:** limited, capped seats at **$399 / year** for launch — seeds
  early revenue and rewards believers.
- This is *premium research-service* pricing — the comparison set is an advisor's
  fee or a data-terminal seat, **not** $5 newsletters. The paid tier must read
  like a research desk, and the page copy says so explicitly.

## Platform

- **Start on Substack** — zero upfront, built-in finance discovery, handles
  payments and the paywall.
- **Migrate to Beehiiv** later if growth tooling / referral mechanics / fees
  become the priority.

## Files in this folder

## Standard (applies to every issue)

Educational, not individualized advice. No guarantees, no implied future returns.
Every performance figure paired with risk and labeled historical. Conflicts
disclosed inline. Credentials described as held/verifiable, not "currently
registered."
