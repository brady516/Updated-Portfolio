The Course

Python for Finance & Algorithmic Trading

A cohort course built on the real thing: ASTI ("Skynet"), the automated mean-reversion screener/trader I built on Python and the Interactive Brokers (IBKR) API. You'll learn to build, backtest, and reason about systematic strategies — and, just as importantly, where they break.

Enroll now Book a free intro call

Who it's for

Finance-literate people who can read code (or are willing to learn), and developers who want the finance side done right. You should be comfortable that most strategies fail — this course is about disciplined process, not lottery tickets.

What we build, week by week

  1. Foundations & environment. Python for finance toolkit, data sources, clean project setup, and the mindset of systematic investing.
  2. Market data & the IBKR API. Pulling, cleaning, and storing data; connecting to a broker API safely (paper trading first).
  3. Signals & strategy logic. Mean reversion, momentum, and why the same idea can be both — building the logic behind ASTI.
  4. Backtesting honestly. Look-ahead bias, survivorship bias, transaction costs, and slippage — how backtests lie and how to stop them.
  5. Risk & position sizing. Drawdown, volatility targeting, VaR/CVaR intuition, and sizing so one bad week doesn't end you.
  6. Automation & going live (carefully). Scheduling, monitoring, failure modes, and the operational discipline of running a live system.
